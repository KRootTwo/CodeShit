Core Components -

1. ESP32 Development Board  (ESP32-WROOM / ESP32 DevKit V1)
2. I2S Microphone INMP441 (recommended)
3. I2S Audio Amplifier MAX98357A
4. Speaker 3W / 4Ω small speaker


Power System -

5. LiPo Battery 3.7V (500mAh–2000mAh depending on size)
6. Charging Module TP4056 (with protection circuit)
7. Boost Converter MT3608 (if needed for stable voltage)
8. Switch Small slide switch (power control)
9. Push Button (For manual trigger (backup to wake word))
10. Resistors 10kΩ (for pull-up/pull-down)


Feedback / UI (Optional but recommended) - 

11. RGB LED (WS2812B / NeoPixel) Shows states (listening, thinking, responding)
12. Small Display OLED 0.96" (I2C)


Connectivity & Assembly -

13. Breadboard (for prototyping)
14. Jumper wires (Male-Male, Male-Female)
15. PCB / Perfboard (for final build)
16. Soldering kit


Optional Upgrades (Don’t buy at start) -

Better for future on-device AI
Better Mic
ICS-43434 (higher quality)
Enclosure
3D printed case / small project box
MicroSD Card Module
For storing audio locally
